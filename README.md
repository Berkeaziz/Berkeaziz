# About Me

I am **Berke Aziz Yılmaz**, an **Electrical Engineering graduate from Istanbul Technical University (ITU)** with a strong interest in **Machine Learning, Data Science, and Data Engineering**.

My work focuses on building **data-driven systems and machine learning models**, with experience in **time series forecasting, predictive modeling, and large-scale data analysis**. I enjoy working on problems that require combining **statistical thinking with practical engineering solutions**.

Over time, I have developed projects involving **electricity price forecasting, deep learning models such as LSTM for time series prediction, customer churn analysis, and fraud detection systems**. These projects helped me gain hands-on experience with **feature engineering, model evaluation, and working with highly imbalanced datasets**.

I am particularly interested in designing **end-to-end machine learning pipelines**, from raw data ingestion to automated training and evaluation workflows.

I enjoy continuously learning new technologies, improving analytical thinking, and applying machine learning techniques to real-world problems.
